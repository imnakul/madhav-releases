## What is Madhav?

Madhav is a lightweight sidebar app that lives on the edge of your screen — always a keystroke away, never in the way.

- **Capture fast** — notes, tasks, ideas, reminders in seconds
- **Telegram sync** — send notes from your Telegram bot, they appear instantly on your desktop
- **Markdown support** — full formatting with live preview
- **AES-256 encrypted** — your data stays on your machine, never leaves
- **Always visible or on-hover** — you choose how the panel behaves
- **Drag & reorder** — organize your notes the way you think
- **Categories & search** — filter and find anything instantly
- **Completely free** — no accounts, no subscriptions, no telemetry

## Telegram Sync Setup

1. Create a bot via [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather) and copy the token
2. Open Madhav → Settings → Telegram
3. Paste your bot token and start the bot
4. Send any message to your bot — it appears in Madhav instantly
